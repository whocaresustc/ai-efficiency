# AI Influencers

A list of notable AI content creators and educators, organized by their focus areas.

## Lex Fridman -- Interviews

In-depth conversations with leading figures in AI and technology.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@lexfridman) · [Website](https://lexfridman.com)
- Representative work: [Lex Fridman Podcast](https://lexfridman.com/podcast/) -- long-form interviews with researchers, engineers, and thought leaders across AI, science, and philosophy

## Josh Starmer -- Beginner Education

Accessible introductory teaching on AI/ML concepts.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@statquest) · [Website](https://statquest.org)
- Representative work: *StatQuest* video series -- breaks down statistics, machine learning, and deep learning into simple, visual explanations

## Andrej Karpathy -- Hands-on Guides

Practical, implementation-focused AI tutorials and walkthroughs.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@AndrejKarpathy) · [GitHub](https://github.com/karpathy) · [Website](https://karpathy.ai)
- Representative works:
  - [Neural Networks: Zero to Hero](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUhRvKZ) -- build neural networks from scratch, from bigrams to GPT
  - [nanoGPT](https://github.com/karpathy/nanoGPT) -- simplest, fastest repository for training/fine-tuning medium-sized GPTs
  - [minbpe](https://github.com/karpathy/minbpe) -- minimal byte-pair encoding tokenizer implementation

## 3Blue1Brown -- Visual Explanations

Animated breakdowns of the mathematical principles behind AI.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@3blue1brown) · [Website](https://www.3blue1brown.com)
- Representative works:
  - [Essence of Linear Algebra](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) -- visual intuition for linear algebra fundamentals
  - [Neural Networks](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDNU6R1_67000Dx_ZCJB-3pi) -- animated deep dive into how neural networks learn

## Sebastian Raschka -- Research & Education

Deep dives into LLMs, deep learning research, and practical ML tutorials.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@SebastianRaschka) · [GitHub](https://github.com/rasbt) · [Website](https://sebastianraschka.com)
- Representative works:
  - *Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)* -- book on building LLMs step by step ([GitHub repo](https://github.com/rasbt/LLMs-from-scratch))
  - *Machine Learning with PyTorch and Scikit-Learn* -- comprehensive ML textbook
  - [LLM research articles on Ahead of AI](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/) -- Substack covering latest LLM research

## Andrew Ng -- Courses & Industry

Stanford professor, co-founder of Coursera, founder of DeepLearning.AI; comprehensive AI/ML courses and industry insights.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@Deeplearningai) · [Website](https://www.andrewng.org) · [DeepLearning.AI](https://www.deeplearning.ai)
- Representative works:
  - [Machine Learning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/machine-learning-introduction) (Coursera) -- the most popular ML course in the world, updated in 2022
  - [Deep Learning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning) (Coursera) -- 5-course sequence covering neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, and more
  - *The Batch* -- DeepLearning.AI's weekly [newsletter](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/) covering AI news and trends
